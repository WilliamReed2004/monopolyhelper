Welcome to the Monopoly-Helper App!


This app will allow you to manage your Monopoly Game without having to deal with all the hassle with paper money etc. And it also makes cheating almost impossible!

Please Note: This project is still Work In Progress. If you encounter any bugs, please feel free to report them on my GitHub! Thanks and have fun!
